Making a Difference - Choosing Invisible Disabilities

Across the globe, the communities facing the greatest healthcare barriers are often the same ones the world cannot see. We chose to focus on invisible disabilities because they represent the most underrepresented frontline of the disability fight—where misdiagnosis is common, financial strain is a constant, and the "look healthy" stigma prevents families from accessing essential resources.

We are proudly representing our families with disabilities in this fight, and are willing to break the stigma associated with it. By always willing to make a stand to step up, educate, and change our systems, we support all of our connected families and individuals.
