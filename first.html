Invisible No More

Our Mission

To empower individuals with invisible disabilities by dismantling stigma and creating a more inclusive and supportive society. We believe a challenge you cannot see should never be a challenge you face alone.

Our Progress →

Our Mission

At Lead 4 Change, we advocate for the millions living with invisible disabilities. We believe that just because a challenge is unseen doesn't mean it should be ignored. By providing direct financial support to families and partnering with leading organizations to share powerful stories, we ensure that every individual is seen, heard, and supported.

Meet Our Team:

Jennica Alixes

Committee Chair

Hi! My name is Jennica Alixes and I serve as the Committee Chair for the LOHS Lead4Change team. I am currently a junior at Los Osos High School, and it is an absolute honor to be able to lead this wonderful group through our initiatives in making a change for our families and members that have invisible disabilities! After witnessing my family members and friends go through their own struggles with disabilities, our goal evolved into providing awareness, reducing the stigma, and contributing resources to individuals that need the most support. So far, we have hit key milestones within our timeline and we hope to continue this journey and bring substantial impacts to our communities. With the help of our partnership with EasterSeals, we have been connecting members with opportunities for certified treatments and therapies as well as inclusion. We cannot wait to see the future of this committee and we sincerely thank you for the support!
